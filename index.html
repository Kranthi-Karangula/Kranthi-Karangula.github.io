BACKGROUND

About

My path into product management did not start with a PM title. It started in RF engineering, moved through Account Lead and Solution Architect roles, and landed in product because that is where the highest leverage decisions were actually being made.

At a Tier 1 national telecom operator, I lead the roadmap for an Enterprise Automation Platform, consolidating 22 fragmented systems into 10 and removing the majority of manual process steps along the way. A 200 plus person organization across Account, PMO, Delivery, and Operations depends on the decisions made on that roadmap.

I hold an MS in Electrical Engineering from UT Dallas and a Professional Certificate in Product Management from the Indian School of Business. My focus now is enterprise SaaS and AI native platforms, where systems level thinking and outcome ownership matter more than a job title ever did.
